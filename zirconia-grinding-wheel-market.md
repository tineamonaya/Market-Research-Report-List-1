<p><p>Zirconia grinding wheels are primarily categorized into flat and conical types, each serving distinct applications. Flat wheels are designed for surface grinding, providing a uniform finish on flat surfaces, ideal for producing precise components. In contrast, conical wheels feature a tapered design, facilitating access to hard-to-reach areas and allowing for effective grinding of complex shapes. Both types leverage the durability of zirconia, ensuring enhanced performance, reduced wear, and improved efficiency in various industrial settings.</p></p>
