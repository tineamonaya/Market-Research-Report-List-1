<p><p>Tumor marker testing encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes in cancer diagnosis and management. Biochemical markers detect specific substances, while oncogenes identify genetic alterations linked to cancer. Growth factors are proteins that influence cell proliferation, and hormones can indicate endocrine tumors. Colony stimulating factors aid in blood cell production insights, whereas lymphokines mediate immune responses. Immunohistochemical stains visualize tumor markers in tissues. Additionally, other markers encompass a range of substances aiding in cancer detection and monitoring.</p></p>
