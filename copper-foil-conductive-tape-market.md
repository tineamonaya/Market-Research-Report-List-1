<p><p>The copper foil conductive tape market consists of single-sided and double-sided conductive tapes, each serving specific applications. Single-sided conductive tape has adhesive on one side, ideal for grounding and electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding. It's commonly used in electronics and electrical repairs. In contrast, double-sided conductive tape features adhesive on both sides, providing enhanced bonding for connecting components or affixing materials while maintaining conductivity. This type is often utilized in precision applications, such as circuit assembly and flexible circuits.</p></p>
