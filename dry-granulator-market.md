<p><p>A dry granulator is a critical piece of equipment used primarily in the pharmaceutical, food, and chemical industries for transforming powder materials into granules. This process enhances flowability, improves uniformity, and facilitates compression into tablets or pellets. Dry granulation relies on the application of pressure to compact powders without using moisture, which is particularly advantageous for heat-sensitive materials.</p><p>The Dry Granulator Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.9% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for efficient manufacturing techniques and the growing need for high-quality granulated products.
