<p><p>Thermoset composite materials play a crucial role in the manufacturing of electric vehicle (EV) and hybrid vehicle components due to their high strength, durability, and resistance to heat. Two primary types are Sheet Molding Compounds (SMC) and Bulk Molding Compounds (BMC). SMC is a pre-impregnated material used for producing large, complex shapes, ideal for body panels, while BMC is a more versatile option suited for intricate components like housings and structural parts. Both enhance vehicle performance and reduce weight.</p></p>
