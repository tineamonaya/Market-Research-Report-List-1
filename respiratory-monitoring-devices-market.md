<p><strong>The Respiratory Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spirometry</li><li>Plethysmography</li><li>IOS/FOT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Respiratory monitoring devices are crucial for assessing lung function and identifying respiratory conditions. Spirometry measures air flow and lung volumes, providing insights into conditions like asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). Plethysmography assesses lung capacity by measuring changes in volume in a sealed chamber. Impulse Oscillometry (IOS) and forced oscillation technique (FOT) evaluate respiratory resistance using sound waves, allowing for detailed insights into airway dysfunction. Together, these devices enhance diagnosis, treatment, and management of respiratory health.</p></p>
