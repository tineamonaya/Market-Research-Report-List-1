<p><ul><li>Welding Strapping Machine</li><li>Fastening Strapping Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual strapping machine market is primarily divided into two types: welding strapping machines and fastening strapping machines. Welding strapping machines utilize heat to fuse straps together, providing a secure seal for packaging applications. In contrast, fastening strapping machines use mechanical methods, like buckles or clips, to fasten straps around products. Both types are essential for efficient packaging in various industries, enhancing product stability and protection during storage and transportation. Their choice depends on the specific packaging needs and materials used.</p></p>
