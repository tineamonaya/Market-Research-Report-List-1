<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Type</li><li>Leaf Type</li><li>Swing Pistol Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile electric vacuum pump (EVP) market includes three main types: diaphragm, leaf, and swing piston. The diaphragm type uses a flexible membrane to create vacuum pressure, ideal for applications requiring consistent suction. The leaf type employs a series of leaf springs for compactness and efficiency, often used in space-constrained environments. The swing piston type utilizes a reciprocating piston mechanism, providing high efficiency and reliability for demanding applications. Each type caters to specific vehicle designs and performance requirements.</p></p>
