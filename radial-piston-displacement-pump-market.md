<p><p>The radial piston displacement pump market is segmented into fixed displacement and variable displacement types. Fixed displacement pumps deliver a constant volume of fluid per cycle, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent flow rates. In contrast, variable displacement pumps can adjust the fluid output based on demand, allowing for greater efficiency and adaptability in dynamic systems. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, influencing their popularity across sectors such as construction, marine, and manufacturing.</p></p>
