<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diode Lasers</li><li>Femtosecond Lasers</li><li>Excimer Lasers</li><li>Nd:YAG Lasers</li><li>Argon Lasers</li><li>SLT Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ophthalmic lasers market encompasses various types of lasers used in eye treatments. Diode lasers are employed for retinal procedures and glaucoma surgery. Femtosecond lasers enable precise corneal surgeries. Excimer lasers are primarily used in refractive surgeries such as LASIK. Nd:YAG lasers are utilized for capsulotomies and retinal treatments, while Argon lasers serve in various photocoagulation therapies. SLT (Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty) lasers are specifically designed for glaucoma management, enhancing intraocular pressure control. Each type addresses unique ocular conditions and surgical requirements.</p></p>
