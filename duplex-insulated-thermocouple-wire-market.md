<p><p>Duplex insulated thermocouple wire is categorized into various types based on its usage and properties. Type E features a chromel-constantan composition, ideal for low-temperature applications. Type K is a popular choice for broad temperature ranges due to its nickel alloys. Type T, made of copper and constantan, is excellent for cryogenic applications. Type J, a combination of iron and constantan, is suitable for moderate temperatures. Other types include specialized thermocouples for unique environments, such as high-temperature or corrosive settings.</p></p>
