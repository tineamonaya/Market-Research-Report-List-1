<p><p>The drainable ostomy pouch market is categorized into two main types: one-piece bags and two-piece bags. One-piece bags integrate both the pouch and skin barrier into a single unit, offering convenience and simplicity but requiring complete replacement for changes. Two-piece bags consist of a separate skin barrier and pouch, allowing for easier pouch changes without removing the barrier, thus offering versatility and comfort. Each type has its benefits, catering to individual patient preferences and lifestyle needs.</p></p>
