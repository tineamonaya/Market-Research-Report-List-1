<p><ul><li>Mass Spectrometry Technology</li><li>Ion Mobility Spectrometry Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Handheld Explosives Trace Detector (ETD) market primarily features two technologies: Mass Spectrometry (MS) and Ion Mobility Spectrometry (IMS). Mass Spectrometry detects and analyzes trace explosives by measuring the mass of ions, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. On the other hand, Ion Mobility Spectrometry separates ions based on their mobility in a gas stream, providing rapid detection and a compact design. Both technologies are essential in security applications, ensuring effective screening for explosive materials in various environments.</p></p>
