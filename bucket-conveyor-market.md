<p><ul><li>Continuous Bucket Conveyor</li><li>Pendulum Bucket Conveyor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bucket conveyor market features two main types: continuous bucket conveyors and pendulum bucket conveyors. Continuous bucket conveyors operate in a loop, allowing for the uninterrupted transport of materials, making them ideal for high-volume applications. In contrast, pendulum bucket conveyors use a swinging motion to move buckets, providing flexibility in handling various types of materials and allowing for changes in elevation. Both types serve distinct operational needs in industries such as mining, agriculture, and manufacturing.</p></p>
