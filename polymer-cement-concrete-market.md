<p><p>The Polymer Cement Concrete market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Polymer Modified Concrete** enhances traditional concrete by incorporating polymers, improving strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals. </p><p>2. **Polymer Impregnated Concrete** involves saturating cured concrete with polymers, increasing its resistance to water and chemicals while reducing porosity. </p><p>3. **Polymer Cement Slurry** is a mixture of polymers and cement applied as a coating, providing a waterproof barrier and enhancing adhesion, making it ideal for flooring and surface repairs.</p></p>
