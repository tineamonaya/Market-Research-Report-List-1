<p><strong>The Payment Gateway Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Online Mode</li><li>Offline Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Payment gateway software can be categorized into online and offline modes. Online mode facilitates real-time transactions over the internet, enabling e-commerce businesses to process payments securely through websites or mobile applications. This mode often supports various payment methods, including credit cards and digital wallets. In contrast, offline mode is utilized in physical retail environments, allowing merchants to accept payments via card readers or point-of-sale systems without needing an internet connection at the moment of transaction, with data synced later.</p></p>
