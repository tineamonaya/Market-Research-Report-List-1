<p><strong>The Change and Configuration Management Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-based CCMS</li><li>On-premises CCMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Change and Configuration Management Software (CCMS) is categorized into two main types: cloud-based CCMS and on-premises CCMS. Cloud-based CCMS is hosted on the vendor's servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and reduced infrastructure costs. It offers seamless updates and collaboration across distributed teams. In contrast, on-premises CCMS is installed locally on an organization’s servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but often requiring more significant upfront investment and maintenance. Each type serves different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
