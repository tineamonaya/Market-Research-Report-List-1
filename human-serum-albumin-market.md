<p><strong>The Human Serum Albumin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Recombinant</li><li>Plasma-derived</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Human Serum Albumin market is categorized into two main types: recombinant and plasma-derived. Recombinant human serum albumin is produced through biotechnological methods using genetically modified organisms, ensuring consistent quality and supply. In contrast, plasma-derived human serum albumin is extracted from human blood plasma, offering a natural source but facing challenges related to donor variability and infectious disease transmission. Both types are utilized in medical applications, including treatment for burns, liver disease, and surgical procedures, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
