<p><p>The Machine Vision Controller market consists of three main types: 1D, 2D, and 3D controllers, each catering to different imaging needs. A 1D Machine Vision Controller processes linear data, often used in barcode scanning and simple measurements. The 2D Machine Vision Controller captures two-dimensional images for applications like quality control and defect detection. Meanwhile, the 3D Machine Vision Controller enables depth perception and spatial measurements, essential for complex inspection tasks, robotics, and automation in various industries.</p></p>
