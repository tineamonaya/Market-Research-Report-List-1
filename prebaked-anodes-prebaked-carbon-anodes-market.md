<p><p>Prebaked anodes, or prebaked carbon anodes, are crucial components used primarily in the aluminum smelting process. These anodes, made from calcined petroleum coke and coal tar pitch, are produced through a labor-intensive baking process before being employed in electrolytic cells. Due to their high conductivity and resistance to oxidation, they play a key role in ensuring efficiency and cost-effectiveness in aluminum production.</p><p>The Prebaked Anodes (Prebaked Carbon Anodes) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period.
