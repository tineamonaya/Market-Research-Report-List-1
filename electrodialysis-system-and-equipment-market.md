<p><p>The electrodialysis system and equipment market includes various membrane types catering to different applications. Heterogeneous membranes, characterized by distinct layers, facilitate selective ion transport and are ideal for desalination. Homogeneous membranes, known for their uniform structure, offer consistent ion conductivity, making them suitable for various electrochemical processes. Bipolar membranes, which consist of cation and anion-exchange layers, enable water splitting and are effective in acid-base separation. Each membrane type serves unique needs in water treatment and chemical processing industries.</p></p>
