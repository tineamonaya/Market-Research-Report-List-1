<p><p>The cosmetic surgery laser market is primarily divided into two types: ablative and non-ablative lasers. Ablative lasers remove the outer layers of skin, promoting skin resurfacing and effective treatment for wrinkles, scars, and other imperfections. In contrast, non-ablative lasers penetrate deeper layers without damaging the surface, stimulating collagen production for skin tightening and rejuvenation with minimal downtime. Both types address various skin concerns, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences in cosmetic enhancement procedures.</p></p>
