<p><p>Membrane Touch Switches (MTS) are user interface devices that utilize a pressure-sensitive membrane to register input. The market for MTS is divided into two primary types: those with rubber domes and those without. MTS with rubber domes provide tactile feedback, enhancing user experience by simulating a mechanical switch feel. Conversely, MTS without rubber domes feature a flat surface, offering a sleek design and easier cleaning, making them ideal for modern electronics where aesthetics and functionality are both important.</p></p>
