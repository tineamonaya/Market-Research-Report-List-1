<p><p>The capacitive stylus pen market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired stylus pens connect directly to devices via a physical cable, ensuring a stable connection and no need for batteries or charging. In contrast, wireless stylus pens rely on Bluetooth or similar technologies, offering greater mobility and convenience. They often come with advanced features like pressure sensitivity and customizable buttons but require battery maintenance. Each type caters to different user preferences and device compatibility.</p></p>
