<p><p>The fiber-laser system market encompasses several key types. Crystal fiber lasers utilize crystal materials to achieve high efficiency and power. Nonlinear fiber lasers exploit nonlinear optical effects for advanced wavelength generation and modulation. Rare earth-doped fiber lasers incorporate rare earth ions to enhance performance, typically offering high output power and excellent beam quality. Lastly, plastic fiber lasers use polymer fibers, providing flexibility and lightweight options, ideal for specific applications requiring durability and ease of integration in various environments.</p></p>
