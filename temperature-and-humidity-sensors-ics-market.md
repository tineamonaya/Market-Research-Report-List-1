<p><p>The temperature and humidity sensors ICs market comprises several types, each serving specific applications. Thermostats and thermocouples are widely used for precise temperature measurements, while PT100 sensors offer high accuracy in industrial settings. Infrared temperature sensors enable non-contact measurements, ideal for moving objects. Capacitive humidity sensors measure moisture levels through capacitance changes, while resistive humidity sensors rely on resistance variations. Thermal conductor humidity sensors utilize heat conductivity to gauge humidity, making them suitable for various environmental and industrial applications.</p></p>
