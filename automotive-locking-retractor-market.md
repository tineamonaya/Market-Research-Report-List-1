<p><p>The automotive locking retractor market is divided into two main types: manual locking retractors and automatic locking retractors. Manual locking retractors require the user to engage the lock mechanism, providing a more controlled experience during fastening. In contrast, automatic locking retractors engage the lock automatically when the seatbelt is pulled, offering convenience and enhancing safety by ensuring proper belt positioning. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and safety requirements in vehicle design and functionality.</p></p>
