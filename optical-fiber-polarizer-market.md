<p><p>The optical fiber polarizer market includes three main types: PM-PM (Polarization Maintaining - Polarization Maintaining) fibers, which maintain polarization for high precision applications; SM-PM (Single Mode - Polarization Maintaining) fibers, combining single-mode capabilities with polarization maintenance for improved performance; and M-SM (Multimode - Single Mode) fibers, allowing for versatile applications by utilizing different modes while benefiting from single-mode performance. Each type serves distinct sectors, including telecommunications, sensing, and medical devices, catering to varying polarization and transmission requirements.</p></p>
