<p><p>The plasma etching systems market includes various technologies tailored for semiconductor processing. Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) offers high-density plasma, ideal for precise etching at low pressure. Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) combines physical and chemical etching for superior control, suitable for a range of materials. Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) excels in creating deep, high-aspect-ratio features essential in MEMS fabrication. Other market segments may include various niche techniques or hybrid systems, catering to specific industrial applications.</p></p>
