<p><p>The semiconductor mask reticle market encompasses several types, including quartz masks, soda lime glass masks, film masks, and others. Quartz masks, made from high-purity silica, offer superior light transmission and thermal stability, essential for photolithography. Soda lime masks are more economical but less durable, suitable for less critical applications. Film masks utilize flexible substrates for specific low-volume applications. Each type serves distinct needs, balancing cost, performance, and manufacturing requirements in the semiconductor fabrication process.</p></p>
