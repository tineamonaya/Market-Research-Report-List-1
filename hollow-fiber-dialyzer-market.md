<p><p>The hollow fiber dialyzer market is segmented into low flux and high flux membranes. Low flux membranes allow limited solute clearance, making them suitable for patients with stable renal function requiring less aggressive treatment. In contrast, high flux membranes promote enhanced solute removal, which is beneficial for individuals with more severe conditions, as they facilitate better dialysis efficiency. This differentiation supports tailored patient care by optimizing dialysis treatment based on individual needs and medical conditions.</p></p>
