<p><ul><li>Liquid Cooling</li><li>Air-cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigeration tunnel market is primarily divided into liquid cooling and air-cooling systems. Liquid cooling involves circulating refrigerants through pipes or channels to efficiently absorb heat from products, providing rapid cooling and uniform temperature control. In contrast, air-cooling systems utilize fans to circulate chilled air around the items, offering a simpler and often cost-effective solution. Both types are employed in various industries, including food processing and pharmaceuticals, ensuring optimal preservation and quality control of temperature-sensitive products.</p></p>
