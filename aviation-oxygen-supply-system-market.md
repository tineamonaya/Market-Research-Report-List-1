<p><p>The Aviation Oxygen Supply System market comprises two main types: Passenger Oxygen Systems and Crew Oxygen Systems. Passenger Oxygen Systems are designed to provide supplemental oxygen to passengers during emergencies, ensuring their safety and comfort. These systems typically activate when cabin pressure drops. Conversely, Crew Oxygen Systems are more advanced, tailored for flight crews to maintain optimal performance at high altitudes. They include portable oxygen masks and larger tanks, essential for long-duration flights or in situations where rapid altitude changes occur.</p></p>
