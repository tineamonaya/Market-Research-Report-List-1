<p><p>Elastomer bellows mechanical seals are essential for preventing fluid leaks in various industrial applications. The stationary type is designed to remain fixed while the rotating shaft passes through, typically used in pumps and compressors for hydraulic systems. In contrast, rotary seals allow for shaft rotation while maintaining a tight seal, often utilized in applications like gearboxes and turbines. Both types leverage elastomer materials for flexibility and durability, ensuring effective sealing under varying pressure and temperature conditions, enhancing equipment reliability.</p></p>
