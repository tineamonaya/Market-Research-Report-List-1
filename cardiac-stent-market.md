<p><strong>The Cardiac Stent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bare-metal Stent (BMS)</li><li>Drug-eluting Stent (DES)</li><li>Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffold (BVS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac stent market comprises three primary types: Bare-metal Stents (BMS), Drug-eluting Stents (DES), and Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffolds (BVS). BMS are simple devices that provide immediate support to narrowed arteries but may require further treatment. DES are coated with medication to reduce restenosis rates, improving long-term outcomes. BVS are innovative options that gradually dissolve, promoting natural vessel healing and reducing long-term complications. Each type caters to specific patient needs and preferences in cardiovascular interventions.</p></p>
