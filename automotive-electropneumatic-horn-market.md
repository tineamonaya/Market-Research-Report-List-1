<p><p>An Automotive Electropneumatic Horn is a type of horn used in vehicles that combines electrical and pneumatic components to produce sound. This horn operates using an electric signal to activate a diaphragm, which then creates pressure in a pneumatic chamber to generate a loud and distinctive honking sound. These horns are favored for their high decibel output, reliability, and effectiveness in alerting pedestrians and other drivers.</p><p>The Automotive Electropneumatic Horn Market is expected to experience significant growth, projected to grow at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period. The trend towards electric and hybrid vehicles is also influencing the market, as manufacturers seek efficient sound systems that comply with new regulations.
