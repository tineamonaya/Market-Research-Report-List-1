<p><p>The automotive diesel nitrogen oxide (NOx) emission control catalysts market primarily comprises two main types: NOx catalysts and hydrocarbon (HC) catalysts. NOx catalysts, including selective catalytic reduction (SCR) systems, convert harmful nitrogen oxides into harmless nitrogen and water using reductants like urea. HC catalysts, on the other hand, target unburned hydrocarbons in exhaust gases, promoting their oxidation for reduced emissions. Together, these catalysts play a crucial role in meeting stringent environmental regulations and improving air quality.</p></p>
