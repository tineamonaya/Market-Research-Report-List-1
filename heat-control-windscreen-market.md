<p><strong>The Heat Control Windscreen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heated Wire Windshield</li><li>Heated Coated Windshield</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat control windscreen market comprises two main types: heated wire windshields and heated coated windshields. Heated wire windshields are equipped with thin, embedded wires that generate heat to prevent ice and fog buildup. Conversely, heated coated windshields feature transparent conductive coatings that distribute heat evenly across the surface, enhancing visibility. Both technologies aim to improve safety and comfort in adverse weather conditions, making them essential for vehicles in colder climates.</p></p>
