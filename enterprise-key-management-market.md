<p><p>Enterprise Key Management (EKM) encompasses two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based EKM leverages remote servers to store and manage cryptographic keys, offering scalability, accessibility, and reduced infrastructure costs. Conversely, on-premises EKM requires organizations to manage their key management infrastructure internally, providing enhanced control, security, and compliance with regulations. Both options cater to diverse organizational needs, balancing factors like cost, security, and compliance requirements based on the unique operational contexts of businesses.</p></p>
